Our chickens receive
no antibiotics ever.

No
Anti-
Biotics

Instead of antibiotics, they receive a nutrient rich diet of herbs and vitamins to grow and stay healthy. This is not the easy or cheap way, but it is the BB’s way.

Our Chicken is
locally sourced.

Locally
Sourced

Our chickens are raised here in Washington State. This allows for a quick turnaround from the farm to your fork, creating maximum freshness, purity, and taste.

Our chickens are
100% vegetarian fed.

100%
vegetarian
fed

They receive a nutrient rich veggie feed with no animal by-products, which bolsters their immune systems and ensures that every chicken remains strong and healthy.

our farms are certified by the American Humane Association.

AHA
Certified

Every animal has a right to a healthy diet, and a clean and safe environment. Our chickens are sourced from farms that receive 3rd party verification on animal welfare practices.

Our chicken is
Fresh, Never Frozen.

fresh
is best

Frozen chicken is often injected with sodium, and flavored water. At BB’s, we believe in fresh, pure flavor, and chicken that tastes like…chicken.

Our farms are
USDA Process Verified.

USDA
Verified

The USDA inspects all chicken, but at BB’s we like to go even further. We only source from farms in which the USDA has inspected every step of the process, ensuring that we meet the highest quality and safety standards.
